How to put your dog to bed.


This video provides different methods of putting your pup to sleep happily.



There are many different methods to helping our pups get a good nights sleep. For example, some dogs sleep in a crate. They are trained to think of their crate like their den so it is a happy place, instead of a place of punishment. Some dogs sleep in the bed with their owners, or some just sleep wherever they want around the house. This video hsould help give you some tips so you can determine the best method for you to help your dog go to bed!
