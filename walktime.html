Want to stretch your legs and bring your pup?


Does your pup give you a hard time walking on a leash?



When you are inside all day on your Zoom meetings, so if your pup! It is important to remember our pups need to get up and stretch their legs. If we keep them sitting around inside all day they can keep all of their energy inside or become out of shape. In order to prevent these negative health effects, make sure you take your pup on walks! The video helps to explain better ways to keep your dog behaving well when they are on a leash and prevent them from pulling away, that way the walk is enjoyable for both of you!
