Why does your pup get really crazy out of no where?


This video helps to explain why our pups might just take off out of no where.



In order for us to get our pups to calm down after a case of the zoomies, it is important to listen to the tone we use when we are talking to our puppies. If our pup senses excitement in our voice, their zoomies may come back. Since we want them to go to bed we have to talk to them calmly and softly. This will hopefully help calm them down. A gentle tone should let them know that we do not want to play anymore. Once all of the zoomies seem to be out of our pup's system, it is time to get them ready for bed!
